Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)?

What is Prohealth?
Hygeia HMO offers an affordable health plan that allows you visit a doctor, do laboratory tests, get free drugs, do surgery and more in over 1,000 hospitals across Nigeria, without paying.
Why should I buy this plan?
1. If you buy, you would not be burdened with hospital cost if you or your family member falls ill.
2. Because you do not know when you would fall sick, and Prohealth is NOT sold to already sick individuals.
How do I know this is not a scam?
We advise customers who are skeptical to get confirmation from the closest hospitals around them.
How do I pay so little and gain so much?
Getting a health plan is not new to people in corporate organisations, in order to cater for the average Nigerian, we have designed a pocket friendly, realistic and affordable plan with flexible payment.
Can I visit the hospital if I have not completed my payment?
Cards are only processed after complete payment, hence we advise people complete payment as soon as they can, so as to give them access to care quickly.
Does this mean it covers all sickness?
Covers MOST COMMON ailments but not all ailments
Can I go to Any hospital?
You have access to over 1,000 carefully selected private hospitals across the country. Please request for the hospital list.
After seeing the doctor, do I have to pay for my drugs?
Your drugs are free to the benefit limit.
Can I go to any pharmacy and get drugs?
Your doctor would prescribe the drug(s), which you would take to the pharmacy, every drug comes only with a prescription and recommendation from the doctor, so as to prevent self medication and drug abuse.
How long do I have to wait to get my card?
After full payment, it takes 14-30 days to get your car
How soon can I begin to access care?
You can begin to access care 14 days after you get your card?
Can someone else use my card?
Your card is unique to you and cannot be used by another person
How many people does the family plan cover?
It covers a family of 6(six) i.e.father and mother below age 60 and four children below age 20
Can I buy for my parents?
Caters for people between age 0-60years.
Does it cover immunization for babies?
Yes, it covers ROUTINE immunization for babies
Can I buy if I am pregnant?
Yes you can BUT all antenatal and delivery services would not be covered until after 10 MONTHS of being on the plan. This is to prevent adverse selection(a situation where only pregnant people buy the policy)
Can I do surgery immediately I get my card?
Surgery,optical care and dental care have a 6 months waiting period.
What happens if I exceed my limits?
Our limts are quite generous and not likely to be exceeded but in the rare case that such happens, you would have to pay the little difference out of pocket.
What if I don't fall sick through out the year?
OUr policies are designed to protect you against the uncertainty of you falling ill, hence the policy is valid for a year, after which a policy holder is expected to renew to enjoy the plan.
Are all policies beneficial to the family?
Every plan apart from the family is directed to individuals.
